Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Talkra ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services, including our Shopify application ("Voice Support AI") and website at talkra.ai.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of our services:

  • Store Information: When you install our Shopify app, we collect your store name, domain, email address, and shop owner information provided by Shopify.
  • Order Data: To answer customer questions about orders, we access order information (order ID, status, tracking, customer name) through Shopify's API. We only read order data — we never modify it.
  • Voice Call Data: When customers call your store's AI phone support line, we process audio to generate transcripts and responses. Calls may be recorded for quality assurance. Customers are informed of recording at the start of each call.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our app, including call volume, response times, and feature usage.
  • Configuration Data: Your AI agent settings, custom greetings, knowledge base content, and business hours.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our voice AI customer support service
  • Answer your customers' questions about orders, products, and store policies
  • Send you important notices about our service (billing, updates, security)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve response quality and accuracy
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data or your customers' data to third parties.

3. Information Sharing

We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party providers for infrastructure (cloud hosting, telephony, speech processing) who are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Shopify: Our app operates within the Shopify platform and is subject to Shopify's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Call recordings and transcripts are retained for 90 days by default. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

When you uninstall our Shopify app, we delete your store data within 48 hours, except where retention is required by law.

5. Customer Data (Your Customers)

When your customers interact with your AI phone support, they provide voice input and may share personal information (name, order number, address). This data is:

  • Used only to answer their support request
  • Not shared with other merchants or third parties for marketing purposes
  • Stored securely with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Deleted within 90 days

You, as the merchant, are responsible for informing your customers that calls may be recorded and processed by AI.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our use of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@talkra.ai.

8. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal basis for processing your data is typically contract performance (to provide our service) or legitimate interests. You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. LGPD (Brazilian Users)

If you are located in Brazil, this policy complies with the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD). You have the right to confirm the existence of processing, access your data, correct incomplete data, and request deletion.

10. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Our Shopify app does not use cookies beyond what Shopify requires.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or emailing you. Your continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@talkra.ai
  • Website: https://talkra.ai
