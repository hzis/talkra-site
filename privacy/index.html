Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Talkra ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Talkra Voice Support application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Store information from your Shopify account (store name, domain)
  • Call metadata (phone numbers, call duration, timestamps)
  • Configuration settings (API keys, webhook URLs)
  • Customer interaction data (call transcripts, voice recordings)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and location data
  • App usage logs and analytics
  • Error and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving the App service
  • Processing voice calls and customer interactions
  • Billing and account management
  • Analytics and service optimization
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers who assist in operations
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and your safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

We retain call records and customer data for 90 days, unless longer retention is required by law. You can request deletion through your account settings.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication protocols

7. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@talkra.ai

8. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, CCPA provides you with the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Delete personal information collected
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Submit requests to: privacy@talkra.ai

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for authentication and analytics. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

10. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with:

  • Shopify (store data)
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • AWS (cloud infrastructure)
  • OpenAI (voice AI processing)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

11. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on the App.

13. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@talkra.ai
  • Web: talkra.ai/privacy
  • Address: Talkra, Inc., Contact Support
